Home

I am a PhD student at IIT Bombay (Chemical Engineering / Materials Modeling) focused on atomistic simulations of hydrogen in metals. My work includes layer-by-layer hydride formation, strain-driven diffusion at α/β interfaces, and building ML surrogates for diffusion barriers to accelerate KMC simulations.

Selected Projects

PCT diagram generation (EAM)

PdH layer-by-layer modelling

Atomistic simulations of layer growth, composition evolution and strain coupling across (1-x)Pd + xPdH → PdHx.
