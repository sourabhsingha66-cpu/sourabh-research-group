Welcome

We study hydrogen storage, atomistic modeling, Pd–H and Ni–H transformations, diffusion barriers, and chemo‑thermo‑mechanical coupling in metal hydrides. Our group works at the interface of MD, KMC, ML-based materials modeling, and thermodynamics.

Research Areas
  • Hydrogen storage thermodynamics (PCT Diagrams, VDOS, α/β interfaces)
  • Atomistic simulations (Pd/PdH, Ni/NiH, MD using GROMACS & LAMMPS)
  • ML models for diffusion barriers (GNNs, SchNet, DimeNet)
  • Kinetic Monte Carlo simulation of hydrogen uptake kinetics
  • Mechanical effects in hydrides (strain‑controlled diffusion)
