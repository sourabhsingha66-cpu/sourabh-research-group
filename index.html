Welcome

Our research group focuses on hydrogen storage materials, phase transformations, atomic-scale diffusion, and chemo‑thermo‑mechanical modeling. We use MD, KMC, thermodynamic modeling, and machine learning to study Pd–H, Ni–H, and advanced metal hydrides.

Research Areas
  • Hydrogen storage thermodynamics (PCT, α/β phase coexistence)
  • Atomistic MD simulations (GROMACS, EAM, Pd/PdH, Ni/NiH)
  • Diffusion barrier mapping using machine learning (GNNs, SchNet, DimeNet)
  • Chemo‑mechanical interface modeling of hydrides
  • Kinetic Monte Carlo simulation of hydrogen uptake
